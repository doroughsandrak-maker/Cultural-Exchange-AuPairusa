Connecting Families & Au Pairs Across the USA

Welcome to Cultural Exchange Au Pair USA. We help caring host families and qualified au pairs build meaningful cultural exchange experiences through professional guidance and support.

Cultural Care program fees depend on your role and location. For host families in the U.S. annual program fees range from $9,000 to $12,500. For au pairs, fees start around 1,380€ (or $1,500 equivalent), but au pairs only pay after successfully matching with a family. Apply as an Au Pair Become a Host Family
