# Cultural-Exchange-AuPairusa
Begin Your American Au Pair Journey  Experience life in the United States while providing quality childcare to a welcoming host family. Cultural Exchange Au Pair USA LLC connects qualified applicants with trusted host families through the J-1 Au Pair Program.  Apply Today | Contact Us
Cultural Exchange program fees depend on your role and location. For host families in the U.S., annual program fees range from $9,000 to $12,500. For au pairs, fees start around $1,400  (or $1,900 equivalent), but au pairs only pay after successfully matching with a family.
